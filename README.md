# 📊 Retail Financial Performance Dashboard

Business Intelligence project analyzing retail sales performance using **Power BI and SQL**.  
The dashboard provides executive-level insights into **revenue trends, profitability drivers, customer concentration, and growth performance**.

---

# 🚀 Project Overview

This project simulates a **real-world business intelligence reporting solution** built for leadership teams to monitor financial performance and identify strategic opportunities.

The dashboard analyzes **retail sales data from 2014–2017** and focuses on:

- Revenue growth and financial health
- Margin leakage caused by discounting
- Regional performance benchmarking
- Customer revenue concentration risk
- Growth momentum and volatility
- Customer-level profitability diagnostics

The solution is implemented as a **multi-page interactive Power BI dashboard**.

---

# 🏗️ Dashboard Pages

## 1️⃣ Executive Overview
High-level financial summary for leadership.

**Key Metrics**
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin %
- YoY Growth %
- Loss Transactions %

**Key Visuals**
- Revenue Trend (2014–2017)
- Revenue by Region
- Profit Margin by Category
- Executive Insights

---

## 2️⃣ Profitability Deep Dive — Margin Leakage Analysis

Identifies profit erosion drivers.

**Key Analysis**

- Revenue & Profit by Customer Segment  
- Revenue at Risk (High Revenue – Low Margin)  
- Profit by Discount Range  
- Strategic Recommendations  

This page highlights **discount-driven margin leakage and pricing inefficiencies**.

---

## 3️⃣ Regional Performance Scorecard

Compares revenue and profitability across regions.

**Key Analysis**

- Revenue vs Profit by Region  
- Profit Margin % by Region  
- Revenue Contribution by Category & Region  
- Top Performing Region Indicator  

---

## 4️⃣ Customer Revenue & Concentration Analysis

Evaluates customer dependency risk.

**Key Analysis**

- Top 10 Revenue-Contributing Customers  
- Pareto Analysis (80/20 Rule)  
- Customer Profitability Segmentation  
- Repeat Purchase Frequency  
- Revenue Concentration Insights  

---

## 5️⃣ Growth Momentum & Variance Analysis

Tracks business growth trends and volatility.

**Key Analysis**

- Monthly Revenue Trend  
- Performance vs Strategic Growth Target  
- Cumulative Revenue Growth  
- Revenue Volatility (MoM %)  

---

## 6️⃣ Customer 360° Detail View (Drill-through)

Detailed customer-level diagnostics.

**Key Metrics**

- Total Revenue  
- Total Profit  
- Profit Margin %  
- Total Orders  

**Key Visuals**

- Customer Revenue Trend  
- Order-Level Profitability Breakdown  
- Revenue Contribution by Category  

---

# 📈 Key Business Insights

- Revenue shows **consistent long-term growth**.  
- **High discount transactions significantly impact profit margins**.  
- Revenue is moderately concentrated among **top-performing customers**.  
- **West region leads in both revenue and margin efficiency**.  
- High Revenue–Low Margin customers represent **revenue at risk**.  
- Growth momentum remains positive despite short-term volatility.
